About The College

Darul Uloom Islamic Dawa College (Hudawi Campus) stands as a prominent institution affiliated with the prestigious Darul Huda Islamic University (DHIU), Chemmad. Nestled in the serene locality of Thootha, Perinthalmanna, the college is dedicated to providing standard quality Islamic education integrated with modern university curricula.

Managed by the esteemed Asasul Islam Sangam, the institution focuses on nourishing high moral standards, multilingual mastery, and scholarly credentials. By combining the rich traditions of classical theology with secondary NIOS training and UGC-recognised university degrees, we prepare the youth to navigate and contribute meaningfully to the contemporary world.

"We strive to cultivate a generation of scholars who are deeply rooted in their spiritual heritage while being exceptionally prepared to address the intellectual demands of our times."

"Education is not merely the accumulation of facts; it is the purification of the soul and the training of the mind. At Darul Uloom Islamic Dawa College, we do not only teach books—we build characters. By integrating secondary NIOS certifications, accredited university degrees, and comprehensive multilingual training with classical Islamic jurisprudence, our pupils blossom into standard-bearers of moderate and intellectual leadership."
Swalih Hudawi Thootha

Academic Integrity

The Hudawi Course

Nurturing Scholars, Building Leaders

The signature curriculum of our college is the integrated 12-year **Hudawi Course** designed by Darul Huda Islamic University. It comprises secondary schooling, intermediate studies, and a 3-year undergraduate degree in Islamic Studies, topped by postgraduate options.

Our program seamlessly integrates traditional Islamic sciences (Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, Tasawwuf) with modern academic disciplines (Social Sciences, Humanities, Mathematics, Information Technology), preparing students for UGC-recognized degrees while ensuring deep spiritual grounding.

Student Leadership & Culture

IHSAS Student Association

IHSAS

Ihyau Huda Students' Association

The Hub of Student Activities

The Ihyau Huda Students' Association (IHSAS) is the official, vibrant body representing the student community of Darul Uloom Islamic Dawa College. Under the mentoring of the faculty, IHSAS plays a crucial role in cultivating co-curricular skills, encouraging literary talents, and organizing cultural programs.

IHSAS provides students with opportunities to develop public speaking (public discourse in Arabic, English, Urdu, and Malayalam), creative journalism, leadership qualities, and editorial training through publishing wall magazines and running student forums.

Literary & Debating

Weekly debate circles and speech training to refine rhetoric in four major languages.

Publications & Media

Manages wall journals, creative writing magazines, and print periodicals written entirely by students.

Arts & Calligraphy

Organizes annual arts festivals focusing on Islamic calligraphy, traditional vocal arts, and contests.

Social & Environment

Coordinating charity outreach, campus cleanliness campaigns, and environmental initiatives.
